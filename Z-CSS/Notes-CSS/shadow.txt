* box-shadow: x-axis[+right & -left]px y-axis[+down -up]px blurRadius-px spreadRadius-px color;

*spreadRadius-px
    Default is same size of div.
    will incress that many px in all 4 directions.
    Positive → shadow grows bigger
    Negative → shadow shrinks


*blur-radius (optional)
    Bigger value = softer & more blurred shadow

* box-shadow:inset - - - - ;
    filp
    to bring shadow above the box, default its below(right&below).
